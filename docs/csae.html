Dadadu's Policy on CSAE

Our Commitment to Fighting CSAE

At Dadadu, the safety of our community is our highest priority. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior that constitutes Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

CSAE is an acronym for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation. This term encompasses any content or behavior that sexually exploits, abuses, or otherwise endangers children. Such content is strictly prohibited on our platform. This includes, but is not limited to, the following examples:

  • Grooming: The act of preparing a child for sexual exploitation.
  • Sextortion: Forcing a child to produce sexual images or perform sexual acts through blackmail or threats.
  • Child Sex Trafficking: The recruitment, transportation, or harboring of a child for the purpose of sexual exploitation.
  • Other forms of Sexual Exploitation: Any other action that uses a child for sexual gratification, financial gain, or any other purpose.

How to Report and Get Help

We empower our community to help us maintain a safe environment. If you see any content or behavior on Dadadu that you believe violates this policy, please report it immediately.

Reporting Within the Dadadu App

Use our in-app reporting tools to flag content directly. Our moderation team reviews reports 24/7 and will take appropriate action, which may include removing content and banning the user.

Use the "Report" button on any post or profile.
